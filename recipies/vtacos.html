Veggie Tacos

A fresh and delicious recipe for veggie tacos, packed full of flavor and nutrients. Perfect for a quick and easy weeknight meal.

Ingredients

  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 can black beans, drained
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup salsa
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Sauté bell pepper, zucchini, and onion until tender. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Warm tortillas according to package instructions.
  3. Assemble tacos with sautéed veggies, black beans, avocado, lettuce, and salsa. Enjoy immediately.
