Chicken Alfredo

This Chicken Alfredo recipe is perfect for a dinner at home. It's creamy, cheesy, and full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 cups of heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 cups of fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Saut√© chicken in olive oil until cooked. Set aside.
  2. Cook fettuccine according to package instructions.
  3. In a separate saucepan, simmer heavy cream, garlic, and Parmesan until it thickens.
  4. Mix the sauce with the pasta and top with chicken. Serve hot.
